ALL FOR THIS GAME FORTNITE

Fortnite is an online video game developed by the American company Epic Games and released in 2017 .

Fortnite offers players three main game modes to choose from: Save the World, Battle Royale, and Creative.

Save the World was released on Microsoft Windows and macOS [c]

, as well as on PlayStation 4 ,

PlayStation 5 ,

Xbox One

and Xbox Series X consoles

| S , and Battle Royale and Creative, in addition to the listed platforms,

also on portable devices — Nintendo Switch , Android and iOS [c] .

The game is distributed mainly through digital distribution in a free-to-play model (except for the Save the World mode);

the edition of versions of the game on physical media is carried out by the Gearbox Publishing company .

Although the Save the World and Creative modes also managed to achieve commercial success, it was Battle Royale that

gained extreme popularity, turning into a cultural phenomenon; in March 2019, the number of players in this mode

reached 250 million. At the end of 2018, the game won the 22nd annual DICE Awards — an Academy of Interactive Arts and

Sciences award , winning in the "Outstanding Achievement in Online Gameplay" nomination and many other awards.
