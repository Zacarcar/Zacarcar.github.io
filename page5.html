ABOUT THE SKINS FORTNITE

While many skins have slight changes to color or clothing, Renzo the Destroyer features five fully fleshed colorways that you can choose between. The default colorway is already a fantastic look with a dapper-looking outfit with blue-purple accents. After the default, you unlock more vibrant and distinctive colorways like the white-gold, purple, neon green, and finally, the Mega Wave style which is a royal gold and black with a red afterglow. On top of the style, you can choose to adorn your skin with a luxurious coat that matches the colorway as well as set the skin to be reactive. Reactive skins change and flow based on various in-game events.
